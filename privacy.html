Privacy Policy for FIT24

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

Welcome to FIT24 ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and delete your personal information, particularly regarding Location and Health Data, when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Data We Access and Collect

To provide you with our fitness tracking features and rewards, we collect the following data:

  • Location Data (Including Background Location): We collect precise and coarse location data, including background location data even when the app is closed or not in use, to track your walks, runs, cycling activities, and distance traveled. This background location collection is essential for accurate activity tracking and reward calculation throughout your day. You will be presented with a prominent disclosure explaining this data collection before you are asked to grant location permissions.
  • Health and Fitness Data: We access and collect health data (such as step counts, active energy burned, and distance delta) via Health Connect (Android) and Apple Health (iOS). This data is used solely for fitness tracking and reward calculation within the App.
  • Personal Profile Data: Including your name, email, phone number, age, gender, weight, and height, which you provide during onboarding.
  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data strictly to:

  • Calculate your fitness activity and synchronize your steps.
  • Track your walks, runs, and cycling activities using GPS location data, including when the app is running in the background.
  • Award "Fit24 points" and rewards based on your verified physical activity.
  • Maintain your profile and position on the leaderboard.
  • Send you relevant notifications about your fitness progress and rewards.
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal, health, or location data to third parties. Your data may be shared with the following services solely for the purpose of operating the FIT24 app:

  • Supabase: Our secure backend database provider, used to store and manage your account data, step logs, and profile information.
  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information from Google to create your account.
  • Health Connect / Apple Health: We access health data from these platform services with your explicit permission. We do not share your health data with any other third party.

We do not share your data with any advertising networks or data brokers.

4. Data Retention Policy

We store and retain your personal, health, and location data only for as long as you maintain an active account with FIT24. This allows you to continuously track your progress and access your historical fitness data. If you choose to delete your account, or if your account is inactive for a period of 2 years, your data will be permanently removed from our active systems.

5. How to Request Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data (including health and location history) at any time.

To delete your data:

  • In the App: Go to Profile > Settings and select "Delete Account". Your data will be wiped immediately.
  • Via Email: You can send a data deletion request to our support team at hello@fit24.global. We will process your request and securely delete all your data within 7 business days.

6. Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Secure token-based authentication for all API communications.
  • Regular security reviews of our backend infrastructure.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to improve our security practices.

7. Children's Privacy

FIT24 is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hello@fit24.global.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your profile data, step history, and activity records within the App at any time.
  • Correction: You can update your personal information through the Profile settings in the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data as described in Section 5.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can revoke location and health data permissions at any time through your device's system settings. Please note that this may limit the App's functionality.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

10. Consent

By using FIT24, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the App and delete your account.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
